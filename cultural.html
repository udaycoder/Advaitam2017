WHO Are we?

We are Students of National Institute of Technology Agartala, studying in 5th semester. Each year the 3rd year students organise a technical and cultural fest since the inception of college. In 2015 the Cultural fest(MOKSHA) and Techno-management fest(AYAAM) was combined into Advaitam.
