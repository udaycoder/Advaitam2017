Samya

Saamya is a movement against the discrimination faced by the people of north-eastern states from the inhabitants of rest of the India. Saamya wishes to tackle the problem of racism prevalent in mainland India against people of different ethinicities (including Nepali,Bhutani etc)

WHO Are we?

We are Students of National Institute of Technology Agartala, studying in 5th semester. Each year the 3rd year students organise a technical and cultural fest since the inception of college. In 2015 the Cultural fest(MOKSHA) and Techno-management fest(AYAAM) was combined into Advaitam.
